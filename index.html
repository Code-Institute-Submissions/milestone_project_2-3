About Us Here at The Repair Shop

What we can do for you

Bring your broken or damaged jewellery to our Carlow-based jewellery workshop for a free and non-obligatory inspection. Our senior goldsmith, professional diamond setter and qualified jeweller have more than 75 years of experience between them. Almost all items can be repaired, no matter the scale of damage, and you can be assured that you will receive the best price possible. Unlike many high street jewellers or chain stores, who have to ship their items off for repair, we repair all items in our own workshop – saving you from waiting weeks. Gallery

Jewellery Repairs by an Expert

Our expertise, experience, and our own senior goldsmith makes Jewellery Repair Shop the trusted jeweller for your repairs. We can repair just about all types of jewellery from broken chains, earrings, and bracelets; to delicate engagement rings and antique jewellery. Whether it be a ring missing a stone, a snapped gold chain, a bent earring, a scratched ring, or something far worse that it is no longer recognisable – we can repair, restore, or even recreate it. Our Instore Repair Workshop

Having our own in-store workshop means that your jewellery repairs will be done in Carlow and not sent off to a faraway place. Our workshop and professional diamond setter and goldsmith, combined with our extensive inventory, makes us your one-stop-shop for all your repair and restoration needs. Gallery
